Hosts Kevin & Lucy Davies are committed to providing cycling holidays that are all inclusive and represent outstanding value for money. From touch down to take off we will aim to ensure all your needs are met to give you the best Costa Blanca cycling experience possible!

Kevin is a British Cycling Level 2 qualified Road and MTB leader, a Sports Scientist, and a veteran of a host of endurance long distance events such as LEJoG, North Coast 500 and Audax. Lucy is an excellent host who will feed you well and ensure your break will be stress free throughout.

Location

Villa Insert name is nestled in the foothills of the Serra Mariola National Park, 25 minutes from Alicante Airport and 20 minutes north east of the city centre. Overlooking the village of Busot, it commands stunning views of Alicante with the mountains as an impressive backdrop. What better place to relax with a sundown after an epic day in the saddle? There is an average of 6 hours sunshine a day even in January and temperatures are typically in late teens/early twenties celsius over the winter period.

Routes

The routes you ride are entirely up to you. Whether itâ€™s a long day in the hills or a flat coastal run, you decide! All routes have been ridden numerous times and risk assessed by ride leader Kevin and graded in line with their difficulty to help you choose. You can choose to use a guide, take a Garmin device pre-loaded with your route, or download GPX files to your own device. Rest days can be built into a longer break and we are more than happy to take you sightseeing if your legs are in need of a rest.

Accomodation

Three twin-bed rooms, all with their own bathroom tastefully decorated in local style. There are various communal living spaces, both indoor and outdoor, along with an outdoor swimming pool when the weather is warm enough.

Food and Drink

Energy packed breakfasts and evening meals consisting typically of pasta, BBQ and tapas. Post ride nibbles to recharge and ride nutrition are all included alongside beer, wine, soft drinks, tea and coffee. We cater for vegetarians and vegans if given notice. All you need is a little lunch money per day, as we stop off in one of our specially chosen stops for a mountain or beach lunch.

Little Extras!

Cycling kit laundered for you daily! Bring two sets so you can wear one, while the other is in the wash. We offer transport into Alicante if you fancy heading out for dinner. Sightseeing is an option if you'd like a rest day.
